Sharing and learning are the drivers of radical innovation.
By sharing ideas and developing projects together, we can inspire the building industry.

Join us for an event exploring open source and open collaboration!

The event, "Open Source in AEC", brings together digital doers and innovators at the forefront of reshaping how we design, build, and collaborate.

Featuring insightful talks from industry experts:

- [Victor Barbosa](https://www.linkedin.com/in/victorwanderley/), Digital Practice Lead @SHL, will share his perspectives on open source and its applications in a multinational design and construction company.
- [Stjepan Mikulic](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stjepanmikulic/), Founder @AI in AEC, will illuminate the intersection of open source and artificial intelligence, showcasing how these can propel the building industry forward.

The event will be concluded by a panel discussion with the speakers, moderated by [Alexander Jacobsen](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexandermjacobson/), LCA Specialist Team Lead @Ramboll.

## Agenda

- 16:00 – 16:05 Introduction
- 16:05 – 16:30 Victor Barbosa, SHL
- 16:30 – 16:55 Stjepan Mikulic, AI in AEC
- 16:55 – 17:20 Speaker to be announced
- 17:20 – 17:45 Panel Discussion
- 17:45 – 18:00 Networking (and drinks)

Don't miss this opportunity to gain insights, network with industry leaders, and be part of the conversation shaping the future of AEC. Whether you're a seasoned professional, an aspiring innovator, or simply curious about the transformative potential of open source in AEC, this event is for you.
